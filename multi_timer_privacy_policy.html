Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

Multi-Timer is developed by BT Design Projects (bt.design.projects@gmail.com). This policy explains what information is collected when you use the app and how it is used. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Information We Collect

The Multi-Timer app itself does not collect any personal information. The only data stored is your premium purchase status (yes or no), saved privately on your own device using Android SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The free version of Multi-Timer displays banner advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may automatically collect the following information in order to serve relevant ads:

  • Device advertising ID
  • IP address
  • App interaction data
  • General device information (model, OS version)

For more information on how Google uses this data, please see Google's Privacy Policy.

Premium users see no advertisements. AdMob is not active for premium users.

Data Storage

Your premium purchase status is stored locally on your device only. No data is transmitted to external servers by the app itself.

Permissions

  • VIBRATE — Used to vibrate your phone when a timer completes.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — Used to send a notification when a timer finishes (Android 13 and above).
  • INTERNET / ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — Required for Google AdMob to serve advertisements in the free version only.

Third-Party Services

This app uses Google AdMob for advertising. AdMob is operated by Google LLC. Their privacy practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

Children's Privacy

Multi-Timer is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
